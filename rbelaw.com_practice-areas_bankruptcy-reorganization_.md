## BANKRUPTCY & REORGANIZATION

Our focus is on maximizing our clients’ recovery in a timely and cost-effective manner.

### Our bankruptcy and reorganization [attorneys](https://rbelaw.com/our-team/attorneys/) have substantial experience collecting monies owed to our [business clients](https://rbelaw.com/clients/\#finance), in amounts ranging from thousands of dollars to hundreds of millions of dollars, in Indiana state courts and in federal bankruptcy courts.

## [Creditor’s Rights](https://web.archive.org/web/20190121004854/http://rbelaw.com/practice-areas/bankruptcy-reorganization/creditors-rights)

**The Firm’s legal experience includes such areas as:**

- fraudulent transfer analysis
- negotiation and documentation of financing agreements, debt restructurings, loan workouts, cash collateral arrangements, and debtor-in-possession financing arrangements
- complex bankruptcy litigation
- counseling with respect to transfers of claims against or interests in bankrupt entities
- formulation, negotiation and promulgation of plans of reorganization and related documents

**The Firm’s creditors’ rights experience also includes such areas as:**

- foreclosures and deeds-in-lieu of foreclosure
- appointment of receivers
- replevin, garnishment and attachment proceedings, pre- and post-judgment
- suits on guaranties
- Uniform Commercial Code Issues
- other commercial litigation matters

### [Debt Relief](https://web.archive.org/web/20190121004854/http://rbelaw.com/practice-areas/bankruptcy-reorganization/debt-relief)

Our bankruptcy and reorganization [attorneys](https://rbelaw.com/our-team/attorneys/) help individuals, consumers, business investors, and small businesses file for bankruptcy protection under the [United States Bankruptcy Code,](https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11) whether it be a [liquidation under Chapter 7](https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/chapter-7) or a reorganization under [Chapters 11](https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/chapter-11) or [13](https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/chapter-13). We strive to provide a high degree of service and personal care to each of our clients. Our philosophy is simple: Provide competent and high quality bankruptcy services for a reasonable fee.
